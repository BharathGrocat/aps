FAQ

Generator FAQs

What is CPCB IV+ and why does it matter?
+
CPCB IV+ is India's most recent emission norm for diesel generator sets, effective from 2023. All new DG sets must comply with this norm. Apollo's MTU generators are fully CPCB IV+ certified, ensuring they meet current regulations and future-proof your investment.
What is the difference between Rolls-Royce MTU and FORCE MTU?
+
Both are built on MTU engine technology. Rolls-Royce Solutions MTU is the premium flagship range for critical applications. FORCE MTU is positioned for industrial and commercial use, offering excellent reliability at a competitive price point. Apollo supplies both ranges.
Do you offer AMC and breakdown support?
+
Yes. Apollo offers comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) covering scheduled preventive maintenance, genuine spare parts, and 24×7 emergency breakdown support across Karnataka and surrounding regions.
